# Quant-Finance

This repository contains a curated collection of models, research workflows and analytical tools developed across valuation, forecasting, risk analysis, and portfolio monitoring. The work showcased here reflects the technical and analytical depth required for modern investment valuation, supporting pre-deal due dilligence, ongoing portfolio oversight and trading/valuation strategies. 

## Purpose:

To present institutional-grade analytics that support:
- Investment Screening
- Valuation & Scenario Analysis
- Operational KPI Monitoring
- Performance Diagnostics
- Decision-making For Capital Allocation & Risk Evaluation

## Valuation & Financial Modeling:
- Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) Models
- WACC & Capital Structure Analysis
- DuPont Decomposition
- Sensitivity & Scenario Modeling
- Three Statement Modeling Frameworks

## Risk Return & Analytics:
- Monte Carlo Simulations
- Time-series Forecasting
- Volatility, Factor & Correlation Analysis
- Drawdown & Performance Attribution

## Portfolio Modeling:
- KPI Tracking & Operational Dashboards
- Cash Flow Forecasting Models
- Budget vs. Actual Variance Analytics
- Multi-Scenario Performance Stress Tests

## Modeling Comparisions:
- Geometric Brownian Motion
- Vasicek Model
- Cox-Ingersoll-Ross
- Heston Stochastic Volatility Model
- SABR Model for IV Surfaces
- GARCH/ARIMA Models
 
## Trading and Market Microstructure:
- Execution Models
- Signal Engineering
- Real-time Dashboards
